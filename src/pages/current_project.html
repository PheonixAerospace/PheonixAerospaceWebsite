PAX-2

In preperation for the the AIAA DBF competition, the team will build a competition-worthy plane while bolstering recruitment efforts. The project will prepare the team to ultimately participate in the 2025 DBF competition!

PAX-1

Over the summer of 2023, Phoenix Aerospace built and flew a starter RC plane based of a a design on Joyplanes.com. This plane was mostly a trial run to familiarize the team with the RC plane material aquisition and building process. Currently, PAX-1 remains operational as a practice plane for Phoenix Aerospace!
