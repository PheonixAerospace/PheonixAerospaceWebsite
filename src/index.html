Our Story

Phoenix Aerospace is a new engineering design team at the Memorial Univeristy of Newfoundland (MUN), established in January 2022. It is the only design team at MUN that works in the Aerospace field.


We want to create more than just a club to fly RC planes. Beyond that, our goal is to create a safe space that opens up new opportunities for students to explore concepts from Aerospace and improve their problem-solving. We build RC planes with the AIAA DBF competition in mind, hoping to compete against other universities in designing a product with a dynamic and exiting set of contraints.
